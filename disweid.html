DisWeiD

IDM - Intelligent Dance Music

About the music

Samling, looping, and life instruments are part of the DisWeiD setup. The genre varies with elements of breaks and varied beats in with traditional house beats and classical lifts or funk changes. DisWeiD is about putting something extra into tune composition. It is too easy for someone to get a sample pack of loops and create the same beats and sounds. "Breaking News: Guy who put drum loop over his favourite track thinks he's a DJ".

DisWeiD adds something cerebral to the experience. The Melodies and varience in the instruments appear to be more classical in parts so as to give an interesting listening experience, but fused with the beats and bass to keep the feet tapping or dancing . All our sounds are designed from the ground up with the help of the studio instruments. And we bring as much of those instruments into the live play as we can.

The Live Set

The set is one long musical experience. Each tune flows into the next in harmony. The live experience comes from the mixing and the live instruments coordinated. The set is 2 hours of dance music. We spend a lot of time making sure the groove is right throughout so that it's more of an experience than just listening to tunes from a DJ or streamed.

Contol of the rig comes from iPad and APC-40 so that we can move around the scenes depending on how we want to move the crowd. Ableton live controls the ins and outs, and the live instruments are blended in at the mixing desk

About DisWeiD

/dĭswīēd/ noun , group of people dancing clearly off their head. Disw-eye-d, or Diswide. "These white boys be dis wide" - André 3000.

DisWeiD is the brainchild of mobri and Idego del Tuna. From a love of dance music, savage mixes, Raymond Franklin DJ, Orbital, Halfmoon Club, Maas and a host of other influences, the music has flowed since 2012.

The duo coordinate from a background in traditional irish music (accordion/piano)on one side to more funky and jazzy stuff from the bass turned synth/keyboard player.
