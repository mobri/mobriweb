The Lough Cafe

The Lough Cafe is a charming, dog-friendly spot in Cork, offering delicious food, great coffee, and scenic views by The Lough.

Welcome!

Cork's hidden gem cafe nestled by The Lough, the picturesque bird santuary in the heart of Cork city. Our dog friendly cafe offers stunning views of the serene lake and its natural beauty. Easily accessible from the city center, we provide a tranquil escape for both locals and visitors. Come enjoy the calm ambiance and charming scenery that make The Lough Cafe a unique destination.

Open most days 8:30 am til 9 pm.
9:00 am to 7:00 pm weekends and Mondays

Dog Friendly

We love dogs! Bring your furry friends along to The Lough Cafe and enjoy a day out together. Our dog-friendly policy means your pets are welcome to relax with you in our outdoor seating area. We even offer complimentary water bowls and treats to keep your canine companions happy and hydrated while you enjoy your visit.

Food

Indulge in our delicious menu. At The Lough Cafe, we pride ourselves on serving a variety of fresh, locally-sourced dishes. From hearty toasties to light snacks and decadent desserts, our menu caters to all tastes and dietary preferences.

Experience the nicest coffee in Cork. Our baristas are passionate about coffee, and it shows in every cup. From a smooth latte to a robust espresso, our expertly crafted beverages are the perfect complement to your meal or a stand-alone pleasure. We do oatmilk and other non dairy options too!

Outdoor Area

Enjoy the outdoors at the The Lough Cafe with our rustic, friendly and relaxed outdoor seating area where you can relax for a chat with a friend while taking in the scenic views of The Lough. Our area is perfect for soaking up the sunshine facing south looking over the Lough. Relax with friends, or simply enjoy the peaceful environment after a walk around the Lough. It's an ideal spot for a coffee break, or a gathering with loved ones.

The Lough

Discover the charm of The Lough. The Lough is one of Cork's most beloved attractions, known for its tranquil waters, diverse wildlife, and scenic walk. This freshwater lake is home to a variety of bird species. Whether you're taking a leisurely stroll or feeding the ducks, The Lough provides a beautiful backdrop for your time at our cafe.

The Lough is a shallow spring-fed freshwater lake with an average depth of one metre and a total area of 4 hectares. It is a wildfowl preserve, particularly for swans, and has been a protected area since 1881. The Lough is also used for coarse angling (on a 'strictly catch and release basis')[3] and is home to fish species such as carp, eel, tench, rudd and perch. The outer border of the body of water at the Lough is approximately 1.1 kilometres (0.68 mi).

Local Art

Celebrate local talent at The Lough Cafe. We proudly showcase artwork from some of Cork's local artists, creating a vibrant and inspiring atmosphere for our guests.
