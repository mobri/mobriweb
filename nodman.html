Nodman

Nodman is a multi- instrumentalist/singer/ songwriter.

About Nodman

He's a native of Roscommon, based in Dublin, Ireland. 3rd youngest of 8, He grew up on the Pixies/Cure/Smiths/Joy division/Depeche mode and Bowie Wafting from the rooms of his elder siblings. Kieran also plays in 'Wee Small Gods'. a quartet out of Roscommon and Donegal.

Debut Album

His self produced eponymous debut album is available now for digit download and on blue vinyl for a limited run. This a 10 track, 40 minute eclectic mix of electro swing, pop, rock, punk with a nod to hip hop, jazz and Electronica. The name 'Nodman' is the college derivative of his childhood nickname - Noddy. Possibly due to his unusually large head.
